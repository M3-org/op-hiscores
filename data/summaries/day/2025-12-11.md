On Dec 11, 2025, the Optimism project significantly expanded Superchain support by adding Celo Sepolia and enhancing game type logic to include KONA for permissionless chain identification. A critical, widespread issue emerged regarding node synchronization failures across multiple Optimism-based chains, with users reporting "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors, prompting urgent requests for detailed client configurations and logs. Concurrently, core contracts were enhanced to support custom gas tokens and the `op-contracts/v5.0.0` upgrade, while initial steps were taken for the `op-interop-filter` service.
