On October 31, 2025, Optimism saw significant progress in refining the Superchain Registry, enhancing test suite stability, and updating deployment tooling across multiple repositories. A major focus was the closure of numerous long-standing issues related to configuration, validation, and CI/CD improvements, alongside initial design work for L2 Contract Management and documentation for Jovian precompile input size restrictions.
