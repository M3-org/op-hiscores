Today, the Optimism project focused heavily on critical bug fixes and infrastructure improvements, particularly addressing stability issues in the fault proof system and P2P connections, alongside significant progress in Superchain hardfork activations and RetroPGF data accessibility. Key achievements include enabling Jovian hard fork for proofs precompile tests and removing deprecated dispute game v2 feature flags, while ongoing discussions highlight the need for robust error handling in `op-challenger` and refined interop transaction processing.
