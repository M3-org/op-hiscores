## ‚úÖ Completed Work
### Devstack Enhancements & Stability
- The `op-devstack` now supports individual dev features, allowing for easier combination and preventing accidental disabling of features ([#17994](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/17994)).
- CI has been updated to properly run dev features through the contracts test suite, improving test coverage and reliability ([#18051](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/18051)).
- Critical bug fixes were implemented to address race conditions in `op-devstack`'s subprocess handling, resolving occasional test flakes ([#17815](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/17815)).
- Monitoring capabilities have been enabled in `sysgo`, adding L2 observability to `op-devstack` for better insights into network health ([#18017](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/18017)).

### Documentation & Specification Improvements
- Detailed specifications were added for several critical contracts, enhancing clarity and understanding of their functionalities within the Optimism ecosystem, including L2StandardBridge ([#843](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/pull/843)), L1Block predeploy ([#842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/pull/842)), and L1StandardBridge ([#844](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/pull/844)).
- A comprehensive specification for the PreimageOracle contract was created, detailing its role in providing data availability for fault proof execution ([#845](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/pull/845)).
- Various broken links were fixed and several documentation files were restructured to improve navigation and accuracy, particularly within `app-developers` and `chain-operators` guides ([#1821](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/pull/1821)).
- Quick fixes were implemented to documentation, including updates to `docs.json` and `reference/contribute/docs-contribute.mdx` ([#1820](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/pull/1820)).
- All references to Kurtosis were removed from the documentation, including `chain-operators/tutorials/chain-dev-net.mdx` and `keywords.config.yaml` ([#1817](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/pull/1817)).

### Acceptance Test & VM Refinements
- Two papercuts in `op-acceptance-tests` were fixed, addressing spammer warning logs and DA footprint test retries ([#18067](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/18067)).
- The Cannon "next" VM handling in e2e tests was reworked for cleaner implementation and re-enabled skipping of "next" tests ([#18064](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/18064)).
- The spammer budget in `op-acceptance-tests` was increased to prevent insufficient funds errors ([#18080](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/pull/18080)).
