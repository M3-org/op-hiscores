On Nov 5, 2025, significant progress was made on enhancing network security and stability, resolving critical dependency issues, and improving documentation quality across the project. A key emerging challenge is the `op-batcher`'s synchronization behavior, which only fetches rollup configuration once at startup, leading to potential brittleness if configurations change.
