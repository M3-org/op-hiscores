Today, the Optimism project saw significant foundational advancements with the introduction of OPCM v2 for enhanced contract management and the specification for Custom Gas Token (CGT) support, expanding ecosystem flexibility. Concurrently, critical discussions continued across multiple repositories regarding a potential bug in the `op-node` sequencer's recovery mode, which could lead to stalled L1 origin advancement and derivation issues.
