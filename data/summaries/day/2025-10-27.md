Today, the Optimism project made significant strides in preparing for the Jovian hard fork with new precompile limits and timestamp updates, alongside a major push to enhance `op-deployer` testing and documentation across multiple repositories. Critical bug fixes for `op-deployer` and CI/CD improvements also contributed to overall system stability and developer experience.

### `op-deployer` Testing & Stability Enhancements
*   The issue tracking the need for more end-to-end testing for `op-deployer` was closed across multiple repositories due to the introduction of many new CLI-level integration tests, addressing concerns about preventing bugs and ensuring full user flow coverage. ([ethereum-optimism/infra#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/docs#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/superchain-ops#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-ops/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/specs#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/op-geth#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/16842), [ethereum-optimism/optimism#16842](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/issues/16842))

### Infrastructure & CI/CD Improvements
*   Updated `op-acceptor` documentation and refined path semantics within its runner module. ([ethereum-optimism/infra#496](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/pull/496))
*   Switched CircleCI configuration to use blobless checkout, proactively addressing an upcoming platform change. ([ethereum-optimism/infra#497](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/pull/497))
*   Improved CI pipeline stability by fixing a dependency issue for `op-e2e-fuzz` job, moving Go tests to regular runners, and de-flaking `TestL2ELP2PCanonicalChainAdvancedByFCU`. ### Hard Fork Preparations & Superchain Updates

*   Updated Jovian activation timestamps for 36 configuration files across Mainnet and Sepolia environments in the `superchain-registry`. ([ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry#1130](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/pull/1130))
