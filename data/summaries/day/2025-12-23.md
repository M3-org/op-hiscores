On Dec 23, 2025, Optimism's development focused on critical infrastructure improvements, including adding healthcheck and drain functionality to `proxyd` in `infra`, and preparing for the U18 upgrade on OP betanets in `superchain-ops`. Significant bug fixes were also implemented in the `optimism` monorepo, addressing port collisions in acceptance tests, while documentation efforts in `docs` streamlined branding assets and corrected operator guides.
