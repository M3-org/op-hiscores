## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue management this week was dominated by a new, high-priority incident and ongoing investigation into complex protocol behavior.

- **Closed Issues:** No issues were closed this week.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Critical Node Synchronization Failures ([#18581]):** A major issue emerged with nodes across Optimism, Base, and Unichain failing to synchronize, reporting "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors. This widespread problem is a significant blocker, with initial speculation pointing towards issues with public snapshots or the need for a client update. The issue has garnered substantial attention from users across the ecosystem.
    - **`op-node` Sequencer Behavior ([#18350]):** Investigation continued into a potential bug where an `op-node` in sequencer recover mode can stall derivation. The discussion centers on how the node requests L1 origins when catching up, which may violate the sequencer drift rule and cause it to get stuck.
    - **`op-rbuilder` Health Check ([#17685]):** An active issue regarding the need for a dedicated health check for `op-rbuilder` saw positive movement, with a community member volunteering to take on the implementation.
