## üêõ Issues & Triage
No issues were closed this week, but significant activity occurred around new and existing issues, highlighting potential challenges in node operation and sequencer behavior.

-   **Closed Issues:** No issues were closed during this period.

-   **New & Active Issues:**
    -   **Node Synchronization Failures:** A critical new issue ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/issues/18581)) was opened regarding widespread node synchronization problems. Users on Base, Optimism, and Unichain reported "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors, particularly when using public snapshots. The discussion points towards the L1 Execution Client failing to provide necessary state data to Op-Node, and maintainers have requested detailed client configurations and logs to diagnose the root cause.
    -   **Sequencer Recover Mode Bug:** Investigation continues on a potential bug ([#18350](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/issues/18350)) where the `op-node` sequencer incorrectly enters recover mode, potentially stalling derivation. The discussion this week involved referencing another PR that might be related to the issue.
    -   **`op-rbuilder` Health Check:** An older issue ([#17685](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/infra/issues/17685)) requesting a dedicated replica health check for `op-rbuilder` saw renewed interest, with a community member offering to take on the implementation.
