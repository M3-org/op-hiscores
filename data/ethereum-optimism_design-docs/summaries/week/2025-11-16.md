## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue management this week was characterized by the resolution of Superchain registry tasks and a concerted effort to clean up lingering technical debt.

- **Closed Issues:**
    - **Superchain Registry (SCR) 2.0:** Several issues related to the SCR refactor were closed, marking progress in chain management tooling. This included allowing chains to opt into hardforks via the `add-chain` tool ([#610](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/610)) and improving CI job summaries ([#644](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/644)).
    - **Min-Base-Fee Feature:** A decision was made to close the issue on `min-base-fee` simulations ([#16958](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/16958)), with the plan to observe the feature's performance directly in a production environment.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Technical Debt Cleanup:** A significant number of issues for `op-node` and `op-supervisor` were re-opened by `ajsutton` due to remaining `TODO`s in the codebase. This indicates a focused effort to complete cleanup from previous refactors, including the event system ([#16917](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/16917)), Interop activation logic ([#16141](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/16141)), and the removal of multi-node support in the supervisor ([#16174](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/16174)).
    - **Blocked `op-node` Work:** Several issues concerning EL Sync correctness and safe head advancement ([#17631](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/17631), [#18124](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18124), [#18125](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18125)) are currently blocked and will be addressed after request/response sync is fully deprecated.
    - **Pending Designs & Docs:** The `fusaka-on-l2` design finalization ([#355](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/355)) is awaiting customer feedback. Active discussion is also underway to ensure the `cannonKonaPrestate` requirement for upgrades is clearly communicated in the documentation ([#18095](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18095)).
