- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Node Synchronization Failures:** A critical new issue, [#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18581), was opened concerning node synchronization problems. Users reported that nodes were failing to sync, leading to L2 block creation failures with errors like "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload." Maintainers are actively investigating and have requested detailed client configurations and logs to diagnose the root cause.
    - **`op-rbuilder` Health Check:** Issue [#17685](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/17685), which proposes a dedicated replica-healthcheck for `op-rbuilder`, saw renewed activity.
