*   **New & Active Issues:**
    *   **Node Synchronization Failures:** A critical issue, [#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18581), was reported where nodes are failing to synchronize. Users on Base, Optimism, and Unichain are encountering "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors, suggesting the L1 Execution Client is not providing necessary state data to the Op-Node. The issue appears to be prevalent for users of Publicnode snapshots with specific client versions (`op-geth v1.101603.5 jovian`, `op-node v1.16.3`). Maintainers are actively investigating and have requested detailed logs and configurations to diagnose the root cause.
    *   **Replica Healthcheck for op-rbuilder:** Discussion continued on issue [#17685](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/17685), which proposes a dedicated health check to prevent `op-rbuilder` from diverging from `op-geth`. A community member expressed interest in taking on this task, signaling potential progress on this reliability improvement.
