## üêõ Issues & Triage
The primary focus of issue tracking this month was a critical node synchronization problem.

- **Closed Issues:**
  - No issues were reported as closed during this period.

- **New & Active Issues:**
  - **Node Synchronization Failures:** A major issue ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/18581)) was reported concerning widespread node synchronization problems. Users on Base, Optimism, and Unichain experienced "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors, suggesting a failure in the L1 Execution Client providing necessary state data to the Op-Node. The issue is under active investigation, with maintainers requesting detailed logs and client configurations to diagnose the root cause.
  - **Builder Health Monitoring:** Discussion continued on issue [#17685](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/design-docs/issues/17685), which proposes a dedicated replica-healthcheck for `op-rbuilder`. This is aimed at ensuring the builder does not diverge from `op-geth`, and a community member has expressed interest in taking on the task.
