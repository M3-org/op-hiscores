## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue management focused on resolving long-standing items and triaging active, high-priority infrastructure challenges.

- **Closed Issues:**
  A long-standing documentation issue regarding the visibility of the theme-switcher button in light mode was closed, with the fix attributed to a recent framework switch ([#1752](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/1752)).

- **New & Active Issues:**
  No new issues were opened, but several active discussions pointed to significant ongoing and future work:
    - **`op-node` Synchronization Failure:** A critical issue ([#18510](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/18510)) where `op-node v1.16.3` fails to synchronize for Optimism, Base, and Worldchain deployments remains a top priority. Maintainers requested more detailed logs and recommended an `op-geth` upgrade to help diagnose the problem.
    - **Devnet Tooling:** Discussions continued on creating a new `op-spammer` component ([#18482](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/18482)) to periodically send transactions on devnets for bug detection, with `flashbots/contender` being evaluated as potential prior art.
    - **Test Support:** An older issue ([#16450](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/16450)) regarding the need for `sysgo` to support flashblocks/rbuilders in acceptance tests was revisited, as it is a dependency for validating other changes.
