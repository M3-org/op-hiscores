## üêõ Issues & Triage
Discussions were active around core node stability and developer tooling, while a long-standing documentation issue was closed.

- **Closed Issues:**
  A documentation UI issue regarding the visibility of the theme-switcher button in light mode was closed ([#1752](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/1752)), with the resolution attributed to a recent framework switch.

- **New & Active Issues:**
  No new issues were opened, but several active discussions pointed to key areas of focus:
    - **`op-node` Synchronization Issues:** An important investigation continued into why `op-node` v1.16.3 fails to synchronize for multiple chains ([#18510](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/18510)). Maintainers requested more detailed logs and suggested an `op-geth` upgrade to aid in troubleshooting.
    - **Devnet Tooling (`op-spammer`):** A proposal to create a new `op-spammer` component to send transactions on devnets and catch bugs gained traction ([#18482](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/18482)). The discussion pointed towards `flashbots/contender` as potential prior art for the implementation.
    - **Devnet Warning Clarification:** A user-reported issue about "invalid block signature" warnings on a local devnet ([#1898](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/1898)) was clarified; these warnings are expected and not problematic for local development environments.
