## üêõ Issues & Triage
No issues were closed or newly created this week based on the provided reports. However, active discussions progressed on several key technical challenges.

*   **New & Active Issues:**
    *   **`proxyd` Synchronization (`#501`):** Discussions continued on how to handle `proxyd`'s ability to "travel back in time" when backends are out of sync. Proposed solutions being weighed include a "multicall" strategy for `eth_call` requests, making `proxyd` aware of "block out of range" errors, and implementing IP-based or signature-based sticky sessions. The potential impact on `eth_estimateGas` and `eth_feeHistory` is also being considered.
    *   **Blob Priority Fee Tracking (`#18102`):** The conversation around tracking blob priority fees in `op-batcher/txmgr` advanced. While a "decay" mechanism for starting fees was suggested as a quick win, the consensus is leaning towards a more comprehensive, dedicated blob priority fee tracker that would be more resilient to batcher restarts.
    *   **Acceptance Testing (`#16450`):** A need was expressed to implement sysgo support for flashblocks/rbuilders to help validate `kona-node` and `rollup-boost` changes.
    *   **Issue Maintenance (`#17743`):** It was clarified that an issue tracking OPCM changes for the `migrate` method may be ready for closure, as the related devstack changes are being handled in a separate issue ([#17764](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/optimism/issues/17764)).
