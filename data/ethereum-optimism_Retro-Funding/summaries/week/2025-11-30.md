## üêõ Issues & Triage
Discussions this week were concentrated on resolving an active node synchronization problem and planning for future tooling, while a minor UI issue was closed.

- **Closed Issues:**
  - A long-standing UI issue regarding the visibility of the theme-switcher button text in light mode ([#1752](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/Retro-Funding/issues/1752)) was closed. It is believed that a recent framework switch has resolved the problem.

- **New & Active Issues:**
  - **`op-node` Synchronization Failure (#18510):** A key active issue involves users experiencing synchronization failures after upgrading `op-node` to v1.16.3. Users provided specific `op-geth` and `op-node` docker image versions being used. A maintainer suggested the logged error may not be unusual after a restart, requested more detailed logs, and recommended upgrading `op-geth` to v1.101603.5 as a potential fix.
  - **Proposed `op-spammer` Component (#18482):** Discussion continued on the proposal to create a new `op-spammer` component for sending transactions on devnets to help catch bugs. Multiple community members suggested looking at `flashbots/contender` as prior art for this effort.
  - **`op-acceptance-tests` Status (#16450):** A contributor inquired about the completion status of an issue related to the `sysgo` orchestrator's lack of awareness of flashblocks in `op-acceptance-tests`.
