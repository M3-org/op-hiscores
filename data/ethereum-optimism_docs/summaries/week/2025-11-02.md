## üêõ Issues & Triage

- **Closed Issues:**
    - **Fee-Related Documentation:** The primary issues closed this week were related to the completion of documentation for the `MinBaseFee` feature ([#1710](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/1710)) and the `DA Footprint Limit` ([#1742](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/1742)), which were tracked concurrently.

A broken dependency in `op-chain-ops` was also resolved ([#16612](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/16612)).

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - Discussions this week highlighted ongoing challenges in core OP Stack components. A key conversation around `proxyd` ([#501](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/501)) explored strategies to handle out-of-sync backends and prevent "backwards time travel" for requests like `eth_call` and `eth_estimateGas`.
    - The `op-batcher` was the subject of two significant discussions. One focused on improving blob priority fee tracking ([#18102](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/18102)), with suggestions for a "decaying" priority fee as a short-term fix. The other addressed the brittleness of the `op-batcher` fetching its rollup config from `op-node` only at startup ([#18178](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/18178)), with proposals to make the dependency more explicit or to trigger reloads on endpoint changes.
