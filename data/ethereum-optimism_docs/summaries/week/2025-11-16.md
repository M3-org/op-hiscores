## üêõ Issues & Triage

### Closed Issues
A significant cleanup of the issue tracker occurred, with numerous issues being closed due to age, resolution in newer releases, or relation to deprecated features.
- **Node Stability & Testing:** Several long-standing issues concerning `op-node` stability ([#16332](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/16332)), state DB corruption ([#549](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/549)), and various flaky tests ([#17966](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/17966), [#18138](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/18138)) were closed.
- **Deprecated Features:** Issues related to the deprecated Kurtosis (KT) devnet were closed, including one for L2 network divergence ([#15782](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/15782)).
- **Superchain Registry:** A set of issues related to the Superchain Registry 2.0 implementation were resolved, marking progress in refactoring and tooling updates ([#610](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/610), [#643](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/643), [#644](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/644)).
- **Feature Analysis:** The issue to conduct simulations for the `min-base-fee` feature was closed, with the team opting to observe its performance in production instead ([#16958](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/16958)).
