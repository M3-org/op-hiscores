This week was characterized by a dual focus on enhancing the developer experience and finalizing long-standing technical initiatives. ## üêõ Issues & Triage
The week saw a significant effort in issue triage, closing out old tasks and identifying key areas for future work.

- **Closed Issues:**
    - **Jovian Upgrade Finalization:** Several issues related to the Jovian upgrade were closed, marking the finalization of key details. This includes updating the finalized bytecode for upgrade transactions ([#772](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/772)) and setting the default value for `daFootprintGasScalar` to 400 ([#773](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/773)).
    - **Tooling & Process Deprecation:** A strategic decision to move away from Kurtosis for local development was solidified by the closure of an issue to support local `op-geth` builds with it ([#16975](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/16975)). Issues related to a deprecated Discord faucet ([#12952](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/12952)) and an old chain validation strategy ([#689](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/689), [#696](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/696)) were also closed.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Local Development Tooling:** A new issue was opened to "Improve `op-up` so it can serve as a sysgo runner during development" ([#17942](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/17942)), directly supporting the move away from Kurtosis.
    - **Upgrade Preparations:** Discussions continue on preparing for the Fusaka upgrade, with tasks for `op-geth` ([#682](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/682)) and `superchain-ops` ([#1206](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/1206)) remaining open for Mainnet implementation.
    - **DA Footprint Limit:** The implementation of the DA Footprint Limit ([#17551](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/17551)) remains a key focus. A blocker related to a spec derivation issue ([#17946](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/17946)) saw progress, with the root cause identified and a fix proposed.
    - **Documentation Bugs:** Following the major documentation restructure, new issues were opened to address broken internal links ([#1802](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/1802)) and incorrect paths in `curl` commands ([#1797](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/docs/issues/1797)).
