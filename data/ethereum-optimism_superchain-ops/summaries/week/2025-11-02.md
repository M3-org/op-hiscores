## üêõ Issues & Triage
No new issues were opened this week, but significant progress was made in discussions on key active issues, and one issue was closed.

*   **Closed Issues:**
    *   An issue regarding the reporting of unused acceptance tests ([#16273]) was closed, as the team's shift to "gateless mode" has made the original requirement obsolete.

*   **New & Active Issues:**
    *   **Proxyd "Backwards Time Travel" ([#501]):** A detailed discussion took place to address an issue where Proxyd can return a previous block as `unsafe` when backends are out of sync. Four potential solutions were brainstormed, ranging from a "multicall" strategy for `eth_call` to IP-based or signature-based sticky sessions, each with distinct trade-offs in performance and implementation complexity.
    *   **Blob Priority Fee Tracking ([#18102]):** A conversation continued on the best approach for `op-batcher` to track blob priority fees. The discussion weighed the pros and cons of a simple decaying fee from the previous transaction versus building a more robust, dedicated tracker that would persist across restarts.
    *   **`sysgo` Flashblock Support ([#16450]):** A contributor expressed interest in implementing support for flashblocks/rbuilders in `sysgo` to enable validation of related changes in `kona-node` and `rollup-boost`.
    *   **DA Footprint Limit Documentation ([#1742]):** Discussion continued on the need to document the risks associated with configuring the DA Footprint Limit scalar and its interaction with gas limit changes.
