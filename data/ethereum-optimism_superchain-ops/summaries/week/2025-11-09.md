## üêõ Issues & Triage
No new issues were opened this week, but progress was made on closing existing ones and advancing discussions on key topics.

-   **Closed Issues:**
    -   A key testing issue was resolved, confirming that Jovian action tests have been successfully added to Kona's CI pipeline, ensuring proof validation for the upcoming upgrade ([#17303](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-ops/issues/17303)).

-   **New & Active Issues:**
    -   **`op-node` Performance (`#15393`):** Discussion continued on optimizing `op-node`'s safe-head advancement to reduce the frequency of `FCU` (ForkchoiceUpdated) messages sent to the Execution Layer. The `kona-node` implementation, which sends only one concluding `FCU` per span batch, was highlighted as a potential solution to the "FCU spam" problem.
    -   **Upgrade Documentation (`#18095`):** The need to formally document the `cannonKonaPrestate` requirement for standard chain upgrades was discussed. The devrel team has acknowledged its importance and plans to update the official documentation.
    -   **System Safeguards (`#18246`):** An issue to improve safeguards around batcher updates on the `SystemConfig` remains active, pointing to ongoing efforts to enhance the robustness of system configuration changes.
