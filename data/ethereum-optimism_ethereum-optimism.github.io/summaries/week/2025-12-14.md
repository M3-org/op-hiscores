## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue triage was a major focus, with several critical discussions progressing on bugs affecting core components, node stability, and protocol design.

- **Closed Issues:** No issues were closed this week.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Critical Bug Investigations:** Several high-priority bugs are under active investigation.
        - A `nil pointer dereference` in `op-challenger` ([#18608](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18608)) was identified as occurring when the L1 chain does not support blob transactions, pointing to an issue with handling uninitialized fields.
        - A critical fuzzing test failure in `OptimismPortal2.depositTransaction` ([#18554](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18554)) when depositing to `address(0)` is being prioritized due to its potential impact on deposit handling logic.
        - P2P connection flakiness between `op-reth` and `op-geth` nodes in system tests ([#18570](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18570)) is being treated as a major bug affecting test environment stability.
    - **Node Synchronization Problems:** Multiple users reported node synchronization failures with "missing trie node" errors ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18581)), particularly when using `op-geth` and Publicnode snapshots. The issue appears related to the L1 Execution Client failing to provide necessary state data.
    - **Protocol & Performance Discussions:**
        - A design discussion is ongoing regarding the necessity of injecting replacement block transactions for the fault proof system ([#18574](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18574)), with debate on whether the Supernode could provide the required data more simply.
        - Efforts to optimize `kona-sp1` continue, with a focus on ensuring expensive operations like `keccak` are correctly offloaded to the host for performance gains ([#18533](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18533)).
    - **Release Coordination:** Blockers for the upcoming Upgrade 18 release are being tracked in [#18548](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18548), awaiting governance approval for `op-program` and final confirmation from testnet runs.
