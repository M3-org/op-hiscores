## üêõ Issues & Triage
No issues were reported as closed this week. The majority of activity was concentrated on diagnosing and discussing several high-priority active issues.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Critical Bridge & Challenger Bugs:** A significant fuzzing test failure was identified in `OptimismPortal2.depositTransaction` when handling deposits to a null address, pointing to a critical logic error that needs immediate attention ([#18554](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18554)). Separately, a `nil pointer dereference` panic in `op-challenger` was diagnosed as occurring when connected to an L1 client that is not Dencun-upgraded and lacks `blobBaseFee` support ([#18608](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18608)).
    - **Node Interoperability and Syncing:** P2P connection flakiness in system tests between `op-geth` and `op-reth` nodes was flagged as a high-priority issue impacting test stability and potentially production compatibility ([#18570](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18570)). Relatedly, discussions continued around node synchronization failures presenting as "missing trie node" errors, which are suspected to originate from the L1 Execution Client failing to provide state data ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18581)).
    - **Protocol Design & Performance:** A debate is ongoing regarding the design of interop replacement transactions, weighing protocol simplification against the explicit needs of the fault proof system ([#18574](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18574)). For ZK Proofs, the need to offload expensive operations like `keccak` to the host in the `kona-sp1` program was highlighted as critical for performance ([#18533](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18533)).
    - **Release Coordination:** The finalization of Upgrade 18 releases is being tracked, currently pending governance approval for `op-program` and a final check for critical issues on testnets ([#18548](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/ethereum-optimism.github.io/issues/18548)).
