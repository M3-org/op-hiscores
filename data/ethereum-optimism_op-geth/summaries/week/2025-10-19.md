## üêõ Issues & Triage

### Closed Issues
Several issues were resolved, primarily related to the Jovian upgrade and the deprecation of older internal processes.

*   **Jovian Upgrade Finalization:** Issues concerning the finalization of Jovian upgrade transaction bytecode ([#772](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/772)) and the default value for `daFootprintGasScalar` ([#773](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/773)) were closed.
*   **Tooling and Process Refinements:** The team has moved away from Kurtosis for local `op-geth` builds ([#16975](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/16975]) and decided not to rewire `interopgen` config, as new tests are handled as acceptance tests ([#17614](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17614)). Additionally, validation checks on PRs are moving to a report-based approach rather than a blocking one ([#689](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/689), [#696](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/696)).

### New & Active Issues
Discussions this week focused on upgrade preparations, feature implementation, and developer tooling.

*   **Upgrade Preparations:**
    *   **Fusaka:** While Sepolia preparations are complete, work on Mainnet is still pending ([#682](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/682), [#1206](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/1206)).
    *   **Jovian:** A discussion is underway on how to handle new fields in `rollupConfig.Genesis.SytemConfig` to avoid breaking zk stacks before the Jovian upgrade is active ([#17907](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17907)).

*   **Feature Development & Bug Fixes:**
    *   **DA Footprint Limit:** Implementation remains blocked pending a spec derivation ([#17551](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17551)). However, a related investigation into a gas scalar bug identified the root cause, and a fix is in progress ([#17946](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17946)).
    *   **Dispute Games:** An issue regarding new dispute game contracts was reopened, as tests are still being skipped when V2 is enabled, requiring further investigation ([#17267](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17267)).

*   **Developer Experience:**
    *   A discussion was initiated to improve `op-up` for local devnets as a replacement for Kurtosis ([#17942](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17942)).
    *   A new issue regarding an error with `mise install` was reported, with a community member quickly providing a workaround ([#17963](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17963)).
