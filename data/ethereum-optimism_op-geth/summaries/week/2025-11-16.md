## üêõ Issues & Triage
The week saw a major effort in issue triage and resolution, with no new issues being opened.

- **Closed Issues:** A large volume of issues were closed, cleaning up the project backlog.
    - **Engine API & Interop Transactions:** Several issues were resolved to better align with Engine API specifications and improve interop transaction handling, including error returns ([#596](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/596)), dropping transactions from the mempool ([#618](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/618)), and preventing invalid transactions in the txpool ([#635](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/635)).
    - **Node Stability & Test Flakiness:** Long-standing issues concerning state DB corruption ([#549](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/549)) and stuck goroutines ([#16332](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/16332)) were closed due to age. Flaky tests `TestChannelManager_Memory` ([#17966](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17966)) and `TestMissingBatchE2E` ([#18138](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/18138)) were also addressed and closed.
    - **Feature Re-evaluation & Deprecation:** Several issues were closed after re-evaluating their relevance. The decision was made to observe the `min-base-fee` feature in production rather than conduct more simulations ([#16958](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/16958)). Other issues related to deprecated tooling ([#15817](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/15817]) and unneeded test parallelization ([#17525](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/17525)) were also closed.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - No new issues were opened this week.
    - Investigation continues into the flaky `TestBreakTimestampInvariant` ([#18137](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/18137)), with recent failures being shared for analysis.
    - Progress was made on documenting the `cannonKonaPrestate` requirement for upgrades ([#18095](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/18095)). A one-pager has been delivered to the documentation team, marking a key step in preparing for the upcoming U18 upgrade.
