This week's development was heavily focused on preparing for the upcoming Jovian hard fork. Work this week centered on feature implementation for the Jovian hard fork and network improvements.

- **Jovian Hard Fork Preparation:**
  - New, more realistic limits for accelerated precompiles were introduced in preparation for the Jovian fork ([#709](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/pull/709)).
  - Following the creation of an issue to track the work ([#708](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/issues/708)), the new Jovian receipt fields `daFootprintGasScalar` and `blobGasUsed` were successfully added and populated during receipt derivation ([#710](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/pull/710)).
  - The Superchain registry reference was updated to incorporate the new Jovian activation timestamps, ensuring network-wide readiness for the fork ([#712](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/pull/712)).

- **Transaction Pool Enhancements:**
  - A proof-of-concept was implemented to restrict transaction pool gossip using a `NilPool` approach, controlled by the `RollupNetrestrictTxPoolGossipFlag` ([#713](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-geth/pull/713)).
