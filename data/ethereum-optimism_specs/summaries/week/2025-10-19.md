This week was characterized by a significant push to enhance the protocol's documentation, with comprehensive specifications added for numerous core, legacy, and experimental contracts. Work this week focused on solidifying protocol specifications and preparing for network upgrades.

- **Comprehensive Contract Specification Documentation:** A major effort was undertaken to document the behavior of key contracts within the OP Stack. New specifications were created for the `MintManager` ([#803]), `SafeSend` ([#805]), `LegacyMessagePasser` ([#806]), `AddressManager` ([#809]), and the `L1BlockNumber` predeploy ([#811]). The `FeeVault` specification was also unified to cover all vault types ([#810], [#808], [#807]), and a new spec for the experimental `ResolvedDelegateProxy` was proposed ([#812]).

- **Jovian Upgrade Finalization:** Progress was made toward the Jovian upgrade. A key clarification was merged, confirming the absence of transactions in the genesis blocks for both Jovian and Isthmus protocols ([#801]). Additionally, the Jovian upgrade transactions were updated, finalizing the necessary bytecode ([#804]).

## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue management this week involved resolving long-standing items related to tooling and validation, while actively investigating blockers for upcoming features.

- **Closed Issues:**
    - **Jovian Upgrade:** Two issues were closed, finalizing the upgrade's transaction bytecode ([#772]) and settling the `daFootprintGasScalar` default value ([#773]).
    - **Process & Tooling Improvements:** Several issues were closed as part of a strategic shift in tooling. The project is moving away from Kurtosis for local `op-geth` builds ([#16975]) and `interopgen` for e2e tests ([#17614]), favoring acceptance tests instead. The Superchain validation process was also updated to be report-based rather than blocking PRs, closing issues [#689] and [#696].

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - No new issues were opened during this period.
    - **DA Footprint Limit:** This feature faces a blocker, as its implementation ([#17551]) is waiting on a related spec derivation issue. An investigation into a bug where the gas scalar was 0 after genesis ([#17946]) has identified the root cause, and a fix is in progress.
    - **Fusaka Upgrade:** Preparations for the Fusaka upgrade are ongoing ([#682], [#1206]), with work on Sepolia complete but the Mainnet merge still pending.
    - **Jovian Marshalling:** An active discussion ([#17907]) is addressing how new fields in `rollupConfig` break zk stacks, with potential solutions being evaluated to maintain interoperability.
    - **Dispute Games:** An issue regarding `OPCMStandardValidator` updates ([#17267]) was reopened, as tests are still being skipped, indicating that the required work is not yet complete.
