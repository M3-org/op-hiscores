    *   Discussions progressed on a potential bug within the `op-node` sequencer recovery mode ([#18350](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/specs/issues/18350)). The issue involves the sequencer requesting an L1 origin block that does not yet exist, causing "not found" errors and preventing the L1 origin from advancing. This behavior could violate the sequencer drift rule and lead to derivation stalls. A potential code fix was proposed, and investigation is ongoing to understand the root cause.
