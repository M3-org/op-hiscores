However, significant discussion continued on a potential bug in the `op-node` sequencer's recover mode ([#18350](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18350)). The issue describes a scenario where a sequencer in recover mode on a healthy chain might request an L1 origin block that doesn't exist yet, leading to "not found" errors. This behavior could prevent the L1 origin from advancing and potentially violate the sequencer drift rule. A code snippet was proposed as a potential fix, but the investigation is ongoing to understand the root cause.
