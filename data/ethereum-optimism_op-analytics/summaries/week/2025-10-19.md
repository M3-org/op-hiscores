## üêõ Issues & Triage
The week saw a significant effort in backlog grooming, resulting in the closure of numerous issues and clarification on active development priorities.

- **Closed Issues:**
  - **Tooling Deprecation:** Several issues related to Kurtosis ([#12952](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/12952)) and a Discord-based Sepolia ETH faucet ([#16975](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/16975)) were closed, marking a strategic shift away from these tools.
  - **Process Streamlining:** The validation process for new chains was updated. Issues concerning blocking validation on PRs ([#689](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/689)) and genesis validation checks ([#696](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/696)) were closed as the team moves to a non-blocking, report-based approach.
  - **Jovian Upgrade Finalization:** Work related to the Jovian upgrade was concluded with the closure of issues concerning transaction bytecode and `daFootprintGasScalar` defaults ([#772](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/772), [#773](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/773)).
  - **Configuration & Contracts:** An issue for rewiring interopgen config was closed as "won't fix" due to shifting priorities ([#17614](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/17614)).

- **New & Active Issues:**
  - **DA Footprint Limit Bug:** A key discussion on issue [#17946](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/17946) confirmed a bug where the first block after genesis incorrectly received a 0 gas scalar. The cause was traced to the genesis SystemConfig, and a fix has been implemented in PR #17947.
  - **Reopened Dispute Game Issue:** Issue [#17267](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/17267), related to updating `OPCMStandardValidator` for new dispute game contracts, was reopened. This indicates that tests are still being skipped when V2 is enabled, and further work is required.
