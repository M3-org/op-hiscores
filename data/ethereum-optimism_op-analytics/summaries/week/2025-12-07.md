## üêõ Issues & Triage
The issue tracker saw the emergence of a high-priority problem affecting node operators across the Superchain ecosystem.

*   **Closed Issues:** No issues were reported as closed during this period.

*   **New & Active Issues:**
    *   A critical issue, [#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18581), was reported concerning widespread node synchronization failures. Operators are encountering "missing trie node" and "Failed to build payload" errors when syncing nodes for chains like Base, Optimism, and Unichain.
    *   The problem seems to be linked to using `op-node v1.16.3` and `op-geth v1.101603.5 jovian` with snapshots from publicnode.com.
    *   Investigation is active, with the community gathering logs and client configurations to diagnose the root cause. A potential workaround involving `snapsync` with `--syncmode=execution-layer` has been suggested by a user.
