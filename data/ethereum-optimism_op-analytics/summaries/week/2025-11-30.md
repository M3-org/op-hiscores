Issue management this week focused on resolving legacy items related to the challenger and addressing active blockers.

- **Closed Issues:**
    - **ZK Proof Generation and Challenger Support:** A major step was taken with the decision to use the succinct proposer for generating ZK proofs, closing the investigation in issue [#18319](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18319).
