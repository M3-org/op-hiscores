## üêõ Issues & Triage
No issues were closed during this period. The focus was on triaging and diagnosing a number of critical new and active issues.

**New & Active Issues:**
Discussions were centered on several key areas, with a strong emphasis on system stability and security:

- **Critical Bug Investigation**: A significant portion of effort was directed at high-priority bugs.
    - **`op-challenger` Panic**: A critical `nil pointer dereference` in `op-challenger` ([#18608](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18608)) was identified, occurring when connected to an L1 node that does not support Dencun blob transactions. A patch is being expedited.
    - **Fuzzing Test Failure**: A critical security flaw was found in `OptimismPortal2.depositTransaction` ([#18554](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18554)), which is being prioritized for an immediate fix to ensure correct L1/L2 bridge operation.
    - **Node Stability**: Efforts are underway to diagnose and fix P2P connection flakiness between `op-reth` and `op-geth` nodes ([#18570](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18570)) and to debug node synchronization failures causing "missing trie node" errors ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18581)).

- **Protocol Design & Optimization**:
    - **Interop Transactions**: A debate is ongoing in [#18574](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18574) regarding the necessity of injected replacement transactions, weighing protocol simplicity against the needs of the fault-proof system.
    - **ZK Proofs**: Discussion continued on optimizing ZK proofs for `kona-sp1` by offloading expensive operations like keccak to the host ([#18533](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18533)).

- **Release Coordination**: An update was requested on the status of the U18 release, specifically regarding governance approval for `op-program` and any blocking issues found on the testnet ([#18548](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/op-analytics/issues/18548)).
