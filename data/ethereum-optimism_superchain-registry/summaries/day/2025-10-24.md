# ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry Daily Update (Oct 24, 2025)
## OVERVIEW 
Today's development focused on preparing for the Jovian hardfork activation across Mainnet and Sepolia chains, updating standard versions, and adding new op-program prestates.

### Jovian Hardfork Activations
*   Multiple chains on Mainnet, Sepolia, and Sepolia-dev-0 are configured to activate the Jovian hardfork on November 18, 2025, at 16:00:01 UTC. ([#1126](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/pull/1126))
