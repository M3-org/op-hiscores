December's development focused on advancing Zero-Knowledge (ZK) integration and addressing critical node synchronization issues. ## üêõ Issues & Triage

### Closed Issues
A significant cluster of issues related to ZK proofs and the `op-challenger` were resolved, indicating a major step forward in this area of the Superchain's technical priorities.

- **Progress on ZK Proofs and Challenger Implementation:**
  - Key decisions were finalized, with the closure of an issue investigating which service should generate ZK proofs ([#18319]).
  - The `op-challenger` was enhanced to support ZK game types ([#18321]).
  - Several related `op-challenger` improvements were completed, including better test coverage ([#18427]), reading from the DGF registry ([#18412]), and dynamically handling challenger bonds and addresses ([#18413](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18413), [#18414](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18414), [#18415](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18415)).

### New & Active Issues
Discussions were dominated by critical synchronization bugs and planning for future work, highlighting both immediate challenges and long-term development goals.

- **Critical `op-node` Synchronization Bugs:**
  - A recurring and impactful issue is the `op-node` getting stuck in EL sync mode, particularly after a restart with an already-synced execution layer like Reth ([#18468](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18468)). This has been reported to block a Base Mainnet node.
  - A related issue involves `op-node` v1.16.3 failing to synchronize after a restart ([#18510](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18510)). Maintainers are actively debugging with users to gather more detailed logs and metrics.

- **Ongoing Discussions and Future Work:**
  - The `proxyd` "backwards time travel" issue ([#501](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/501)), where out-of-sync backends can cause `proxyd` to serve stale blocks, remains active. A plan was established to prioritize a fix starting in January.
  - A new `op-spammer` component for devnet deployments is under discussion ([#18482](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18482)), with community members referencing prior art to inform the design.
  - A new issue was opened to track a webhook integration ([#1175](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/1175)).
