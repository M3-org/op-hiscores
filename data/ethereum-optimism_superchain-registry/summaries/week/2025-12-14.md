## üêõ Issues & Triage
The week saw no new pull requests or issues opened, with activity concentrated on diagnosing and discussing existing high-priority issues.

- **Closed Issues:** No major issues were resolved this week. Issue [#17348](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/17348), related to fault proof system testing, was briefly closed but then reopened, indicating that work remains ongoing.

- **New & Active Issues:** Discussions on active issues highlighted several critical areas requiring attention:
    - **Critical Bugs & Stability:**
        - A critical bug in `OptimismPortal2.depositTransaction` ([#18554](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18554)) was identified through fuzz testing, where a null address causes a failure. The issue has been recommended for immediate prioritization and security review.
        - An `op-challenger` panic ([#18608](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18608)) was confirmed to occur when running against an L1 that has not upgraded to Dencun, as it attempts to access the non-existent `BlobBaseFee` field.
        - P2P connection flakiness between `op-reth` and `op-geth` ([#18570](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18570)) continues to impact system test stability, with calls to prioritize an investigation into implementation differences.
    - **Protocol Design & Releases:**
        - Discussion on interoperability ([#18574](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18574)) affirmed the necessity of an additional transaction for the proof system to function correctly.
        - The finalization of the U18 releases ([#18548](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18548)) is being tracked, pending governance approval for `op-program`.
    - **Node Operations & Performance:**
        - Investigation continues into node synchronization failures ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18581)), with current evidence pointing towards issues with L1 Execution Client data.
        - The effort to optimize `kona-sp1` for ZK Proofs ([#18533](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18533)) is ongoing, with a focus on identifying all expensive operations that need to be offloaded to the host.
