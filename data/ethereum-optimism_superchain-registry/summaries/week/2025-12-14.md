## üêõ Issues & Triage
No new issues were opened, but significant discussion and triage occurred on existing ones, highlighting several critical areas needing attention.

- **Closed Issues:** An issue related to fault proof system testing ([#17348](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/17348)) was closed but immediately reopened due to an outstanding task, resulting in no net resolved issues.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Critical Bugs Identified:** Two issues were flagged as critical. A fuzzing test failure in `OptimismPortal2.depositTransaction` when the target is `address(0)` ([#18554](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18554)) was prioritized for an immediate fix. Separately, P2P connection flakiness between `op-reth` and `op-geth` nodes ([#18570](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18570)) was deemed a major bug impacting client diversity.
    - **Node Stability and Compatibility:** Discussions continued on several stability problems. A user reported "missing trie node" errors, pointing to a potential node synchronization issue with the L1 Execution Client ([#18581](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18581)). Another report detailed a panic in `op-challenger` when connected to an L1 that does not support `blobBaseFee`, likely due to an uninitialized field ([#18608](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18608)).
    - **Future Upgrades and Optimizations:** Progress on the Upgrade 18 release is being tracked in [#18548](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18548), with updates requested on governance approval. Discussions on optimizing `kona-sp1` for ZK Proofs by offloading expensive operations to the host continued in [#18533](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18533).
    - **Proof System Design:** A discussion regarding the necessity of injected replacement transactions for the proof system concluded that the additional transaction is critical for preventing honest challengers from being overwhelmed ([#18574](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18574)).
