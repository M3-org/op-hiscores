## üêõ Issues & Triage
This week saw the resolution of several documentation and node operation issues, while significant investigation began on complex, active bugs.

- **Closed Issues:**
  - **Documentation:** Key documentation gaps were filled by updating the JSON-RPC API documentation ([#1773]) and adding missing environment variable descriptions for `op-node` and `op-geth` flags ([#1874]).
  - **Node Operation:** An `op-node` synchronization failure reported after an upgrade was resolved; the fix involved upgrading and resyncing `op-geth` ([#18510]).

- **New & Active Issues:**
  - **Node Stability Investigations:** Discussions intensified around several critical issues.
    - **`op-node` Sequencer Bug ([#18350]):** Investigation continues into a potential bug in the sequencer recovery mode that could lead to derivation stalls. The team is analyzing related pull requests to understand the root cause.
    - **Node Synchronization Failures ([#18581]):** A high-priority issue emerged with multiple users reporting nodes failing to sync across Base, Optimism, and Unichain, particularly when using public snapshots. Maintainers are actively collecting logs and configurations to diagnose the problem.
  - **Future Work & Refactoring:**
    - **`op-rbuilder` Healthcheck ([#17685]):** A proposal for a dedicated healthcheck to ensure `op-rbuilder` does not diverge from `op-geth` gained momentum, with a contributor expressing interest in implementing it.
    - **Batcher Simplification ([#18479]):** Discussions progressed on refactoring the batcher by removing the `isPectra` flag, with a contributor volunteering to take on the task.
