## üêõ Issues & Triage
No new issues were opened this week, but significant progress was made on closing old issues and advancing active investigations.

- **Closed Issues:** A large number of issues were closed, reflecting a period of cleanup and finalization:
    - **Process Refinement:** The chain validation process was shifted from a blocking check on pull requests to a more flexible report-based approach, leading to the closure of issues [#689](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/689) and [#696](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/696).
    - **Jovian Upgrade Finalization:** Tasks related to the previous Jovian upgrade, such as updating transaction bytecodes and default values, were completed and closed ([#772](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/772), [#773](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/773)).
    - **Tooling & CI:** The issue to create a daily codegen job ([#979](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/979)) was closed following its implementation.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **Fusaka Upgrade Preparations:** Discussions continue on preparing for the Fusaka upgrade. Work on `op-geth` is complete for Sepolia but pending for Mainnet ([#682](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/682)). Similarly, a task to update the absolute prestate has been executed on Sepolia, with discussion on whether to keep the ticket open to track the Mainnet task ([#1206](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/1206)).
    - **Active Investigations:** Progress was made on investigating a DA footprint limit issue, with the root cause identified and a fix in progress ([#17946](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/17946)). An issue regarding dispute game contract updates was reopened as tests are still being skipped, indicating further work is needed ([#17267](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/17267)).
    - **Developer Tooling:** A discussion on an `mise install` error ([#17963](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/17963)) identified a problem with the latest version of `mise` and provided a workaround for developers.
