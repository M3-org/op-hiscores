    - **`op-node` Performance:** Active discussion continues on issue [#15393](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/15393) regarding the reduction of excessive FCU (Fork-Choice Updated) messages, or "FCU spam," in `op-node`.

    - **System Safeguards:** The need to improve safeguards when updating the batcher on the SystemConfig is under review, with connections being made to broader safety improvement efforts ([#18246](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/superchain-registry/issues/18246)).
