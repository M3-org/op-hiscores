## üêõ Issues & Triage
Discussions this week surfaced key areas for improvement in core services, while a couple of older issues were resolved.

- **Closed Issues:**
  - An issue regarding reporting unused acceptance tests was closed as the team has largely moved to a "gateless mode" for testing, making the original requirement obsolete ([#16273]).
  - A broken dependency issue in `op-chain-ops` was resolved, unblocking related development ([#16612]).

- **New & Active Issues:**
  - **`op-batcher` Enhancements:** Two key discussions focused on the `op-batcher`.
    - A new issue was opened concerning the batcher's brittle behavior of only fetching the rollup configuration once at startup. Suggestions include making the config an explicit operator-provided parameter or having it reload when the active endpoint changes ([#18178]).
    - Discussion continued on improving blob priority fee tracking, with a proposal to have the starting fee "decay" from the previous transaction's fee as a "quick win" to better handle backlogs, though its limitations were also noted ([#18102]).
  - **`proxyd` Time Travel Problem:** A significant discussion continued around mitigating "backwards time travel" when backends are out of sync. Proposed solutions include a "multicall" strategy, making `proxyd` aware of "block out of range" errors, and implementing sticky sessions. The potential impact on `eth_estimateGas` and `eth_feeHistory` remains a key concern ([#501]).
  - **Acceptance Testing:** The need to add support for flashblocks/rbuilders to `op-acceptance-tests` was reiterated to help validate `kona-node` and `rollup-boost` changes ([#16450]).
