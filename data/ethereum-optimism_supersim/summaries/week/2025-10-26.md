## üêõ Issues & Triage
No issues were closed this week. Discussions were concentrated on several active issues impacting deployment, testing, and L3 configuration.

- **New & Active Issues:**
    - **L3 Configuration Simplification:** In issue [#18070](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/supersim/issues/18070), discussion continued on enabling `op-node` and `op-program` to handle L3s without explicit L1 configuration. The latest proposal suggests the system should automatically detect if the configured "L1" is a known L2, thereby applying L3 behavior dynamically.
    - **Sepolia Deployment Failures:** A significant discussion on issue [#17912](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/supersim/issues/17912) addressed `op-deployer` failures on Sepolia. It was confirmed that the Fusaka hardfork enforces a transaction gas limit cap (EIP-7825) that causes large deployment transactions to be rejected. Suggestions include breaking down transactions or upgrading from the older `op-deployer:v0.2.6` to a more recent version.
    - **Acceptance Testing Blockers:** A conversation in issue [#469](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/supersim/issues/469) highlighted ongoing challenges in running acceptance tests outside the monorepo. It was noted that private fields in the orchestrator prevent necessary modifications to devnets, acting as a blocker for more flexible testing setups.
