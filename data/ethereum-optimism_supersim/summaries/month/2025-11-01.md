## üêõ Issues & Triage
Issue management this month focused on improving operational procedures and debating future architectural patterns.

- **Closed Issues:** An important issue regarding low batcher throughput ([#16959](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/supersim/issues/16959)) was closed. The resolution was the creation of a runbook to guide responses when the `MinBaseFee` is set too high, which could otherwise prevent transactions from being included.

- **New & Active Issues:** While no new major issues were opened, a significant architectural discussion is ongoing in issue [#18347](https://github.com/ethereum-optimism/supersim/issues/18347). The debate centers on the dependency of `IFaultDisputeGame` on `AnchorStateRegistry`. Contributors are weighing the necessity of a specific sanity check against the goal of keeping components loosely coupled, a decision that will impact the future design of the dispute game system.
